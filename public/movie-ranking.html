The Best Movies Acoording to Sameer

My top 3 movies of all time

Yeh Jawani Hai Diwani

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" is a vibrant Bollywood film about love, friendship, and self-discovery, following the journey of four friends as they navigate relationships and chase their dreams.

Sanam Teri Kasam

"Sanam Teri Kasam" is an emotional love story of two opposites, a misunderstood man and a traditional woman, whose fates intertwine through heartbreak, redemption, and tragic romance.

Aashiqui 2

"Aashiqui 2" is a heartwrenching musical love story that explores the tumultuous relationship between a struggling singer and his muse, set against the backdrop of passion, sacrifice, and redemption.
